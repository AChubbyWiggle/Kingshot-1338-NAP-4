# NAP 4 Rules Server 1338

## Introduction

NAP 4 is a non-aggression pact between alliances **AVG**, **DVS**, **ECH**, and **HWK** in the game. These rules outline protections, shared resources, and conduct to ensure fair play during the season.

## Main City and Farm Rules

- No Tile hits between NAP alliances; a main city attack counts as a NAP breach for the whole alliance until the offending player is kicked and banned from rejoining.  
- Farm protection applies only if the farm alliance is explicitly declared; undeclared farm tags are not covered by NAP.  
- Name changes for protected farms must be notified 24 hours in advance.  
- If you hit a protected farm, you must repay all resources lost (including troop healings/losses) plus 50%.  
- If the farm alliance is not declared, then it is not a farm or covered by the NAP.

## Sanctuaries, Forts, and Recruiting

- We can split 3 sanctuaries (**static**) each.  
- 1 **static** fortress each.  
- No recruiting among the 4 NAP alliances.  

- Alliances will split 3 **static** sanctuaries and 1 static fortress, and each alliance keeps to its assigned sanctuaries/forts for the season (no rotating).  
- There is coordination on which specific sanctuary numbers each alliance takes so sign-ups do not overlap.
- **Static** = You select certain sanctuary numbers and stay with those for the season.  

## Dropping Tags and Alliance Hopping

- Dropping tags to attack NAP members is not allowed.  
- Anyone who drops tag to hit and then tries to rejoin a NAP alliance gets a 1-week timeout before re-acceptance, with possible debuffs from king.  
- Alliance hopping is discouraged; players moving between NAP alliances in bad faith is treated as a problem.

## Events and Tile Hits

- Future all-out/KE/KvK style events will likely keep old rules of free tile hits between the three NAP alliances.  
- City hits will still be restricted unless pre-arranged 1v1s are agreed.  
- Events such as Kings Castle and KvK will have dedicated Discord talk channels for NAP alliances to collaborate on.

## Kings Castle and King Appointments

- Kings Castle is to be shared by top 2 alliances in rotation, with the advisor role being provided to the other alliance.  
- King appointments will be open to all.  
- Shifting appointments can only be done 12 hours or more in advance.  
- Shifting appointments is only done when the advisor or current king gets notified that the person cannot do the specific time; no one within 12 hours will be affected.  
- Farms and/or alt accounts will be pushed back at discretion depending on how busy the application list is.

## Conduct and Breach Handling

- Insults will not be tolerated between NAP alliances.  
- If there is an issue between 2 alliances, the remaining alliances will work as a panel to assess the situation.  
- If 1 alliance is found to be guilty of breaching NAP, the remaining alliances are free to attack.  
- Spying in other alliances is considered a hostile action and is a breach of NAP rules.
